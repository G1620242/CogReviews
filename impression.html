HTC Vive

We got early access to the HTC Vive our first impressions are that it is definitely something special the combination of the headset and the controllers make for some unique experiences that could not be pulled off otherwise this is something you should keep your eye on.

Squad

We had a chance to try out the new early access to the realist military shooter known as squad it takes a unique spin on military warfare with teamwork being the key to doing anything in the world once the game os released we would recommend giving it a look.

Nvidia GTX 1080

If you are looking for a graphics card this is the one to get when we were running games like battlefield it was running it above 60fps and on other game would reach over 100 running the game flawlessly this is worth a look if you need a new graphics card.

Intel Core i7 7700K

Intels new processor performs well being the new processor to have to highest clock speed at 5.0Ghz and a 4k HEVC encode/decode acceleration this is something to look at if you have the money and know enough about the processor.
